# Brand Guidelines - Raj Mind Health Services

## Brand Strategy
**Core Values:** Empathy, Trust, Calm, Growth.
**Mission:** To provide a conducive environment for children with learning disabilities and offer professional mental health services with a human touch.
**Visual Direction:** Transition from a high-contrast, energetic palette (Purple/Orange) to a more serene, professional, and calming palette (Teal/Sage/Mint) while retaining the legacy Orange as a warm accent.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Trust & Calm)
- **Deep Teal**
  - **Hex:** `#264653`
  - **Usage:** Headings, Footer Background, Navbar Background, Primary Text.
  - **Psychology:** Represents stability, intelligence, and trust. Replaces the legacy Dark Purple.

- **Sage Green**
  - **Hex:** `#2A9D8F`
  - **Usage:** Primary Buttons, Subheadings, Active States, Icons.
  - **Psychology:** Represents growth, harmony, and health.

### Secondary Colors (Warmth & Balance)
- **Soft Mint**
  - **Hex:** `#E9F5F9`
  - **Usage:** Page Backgrounds (Alternating sections), Hero Backgrounds.
  - **Psychology:** Creates a sense of openness and airiness. Replaces the legacy Light Purple.

- **Warm Cream**
  - **Hex:** `#F8F9FA` (or `#FFF1E6` for warmer tone)
  - **Usage:** Main Content Backgrounds, Card Backgrounds.
  - **Psychology:** Clean but not sterile.

### Accent Colors (Energy & Legacy)
- **Burnt Orange**
  - **Hex:** `#E76F51` (or `#F4A261` for lighter)
  - **Usage:** Call-to-Action (CTA) Hover states, Important Highlights, "Book Now" buttons.
  - **Psychology:** Energy, warmth, and optimism. A nod to the legacy Orange brand color.

## Typography
*Current Font Stack (Legacy):* Oswald, Paprika, Lato.
*Recommendation for Upgrade:*
- **Headings:** Oswald (Keep for continuity) or transition to **Montserrat** for a more modern feel.
- **Body:** Lato (Keep) or **Open Sans** / **Inter** for better readability on screens.
